# Sarhny Child Safety Standards

Sarhny is a social app for adults. The service is not intended for children, and accounts identified as belonging to minors are removed.

## Prohibited Content

Sarhny does not allow child sexual abuse material, sexual exploitation of minors, grooming, solicitation of minors, content that targets minors, or any content that exposes or endangers a child.

## Reporting

Users can report posts, messages, and accounts from inside the app. Reports related to child safety are reviewed and acted on as a priority.

## Enforcement

Sarhny may remove content, restrict features, suspend accounts, or permanently delete accounts that violate these standards.

## Legal Compliance

Sarhny complies with applicable child safety laws and reports child sexual abuse material or credible child safety concerns to the appropriate regional and national authorities when required.
